Panzersimulator

Description

VR simulation for operating a Tiger tank from World War II. The focus was on immersion, interaction, and realistic operating logic. To really demonstrate what it’s like to operate such a large vehicle, we’ve built a replica based on the public construction documents and consulted the official manual for the controls. Almost all of the interoir of the tank was modelled by me and my partner.
